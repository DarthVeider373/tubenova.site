About the devotion

A Catholic tradition of faith and trust in Mary's intercession

The devotion to Our Lady Undoer of Knots originated in Germany in the seventeenth century and is practised by faithful people around the world. Pope Francis helped spread this devotion throughout Latin America.

"Virgin Mary, Mother of beautiful love, Mother who has never abandoned a child who cried for help, help me to trust in God's plan for my life."

This material was prepared to guide your 9 days of prayer with thoughtful reflections and traditional devotional prayers.
